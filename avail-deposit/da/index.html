Build Seamless Experiences

Avail DA empowers developers to focus on product experience, not infrastructure. With predictable performance and instant data verification, your apps can deliver faster finality, smoother onboarding, and uninterrupted user flows — even under heavy load.

Whether you're building DeFi platforms, gaming worlds, or real-world asset markets, Avail's DA layer ensures your users get the speed, security, and reliability they expect from modern on-chain applications.

USP

Verifiable by Design

Avail DA guarantees data integrity through a combination of validity proofs, KZG polynomial commitments, and data availability sampling (DAS). Every block posted to Avail can be independently verified by light clients, even on resource-constrained devices, without needing to download the entire dataset.

This means applications built on Avail inherit cryptographic proof of data availability, enabling trustless interoperability and secure rollup settlement across L2s and L3s.
